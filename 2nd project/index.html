Our Story

Coffee is not
a drink. It's a mood.

NOIR was born from a single obsession: the perfect cup. We source micro-lot beans from twelve regions worldwide, roasted in-house on vintage cast-iron drums that have aged as beautifully as our craft.

Every pour is intentional. Every blend is a chapter. We invite you into the ritual.
